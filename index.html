About Shobha Pustakalay

Shobha Pustakalay & Stationery is a trusted neighbourhood stationery shop located in the heart of Bajrang Nagar, Manewada, Nagpur. We have been serving students, schools, and families with quality stationery products for years.

Our mission is simple — to provide everything a student needs under one roof, at one reliable place. From daily-use pens and notebooks to complete Navneet book collections, we are committed to supporting academic excellence in our community.
